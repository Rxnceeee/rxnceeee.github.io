This is where your story as a mariner starts. Focus on your foundations, survive Math, make good friends, and get used to waking up early. This year shapes your whole mindset as a future officer.

Maritime English Trigonometry Roam Advance Rotation Chemistry Cook healthy food para kay corki Physics for Mariners Physical Education NSTP Computer Applications Basic Seamanship

2nd Year · 2027–2028
BSMarT Second Year Cadet
Navigation Begins 🧭
You start learning how to actually read the ocean. Charts, stars, and weather become your language. STCW Basic Safety Training kicks in this year — it is required on every vessel in the world.
What you will study
Chart Navigation Terrestrial Navigation Ship Stability I Meteorology I Marine Engineering Basics Cargo Handling Intro STCW Basic Safety Swimming & Survival
STCW Basic Safety Training First Aid at Sea Getting Serious 🔥
🖤 "Chart work is annoying at first — draw every line clean. Sloppy charts cost lives. Take your time on this one, I promise it will click." — Prof. Kuromi
3rd Year · 2028–2029
BSMarT Third Year Cadet
Advanced Bridge Operations 🌐
This is where it gets intense. RADAR, GMDSS, celestial navigation, Rules of the Road. You will start practicing watchkeeping like a real officer. This is the year that separates who is serious from who is not.
What you will study
Celestial Navigation RADAR / ARPA GMDSS Communications COLREGS Rules of the Road Ship Stability II Cargo Operations Watchkeeping Procedures Maritime Law Meteorology II
RADAR / ARPA Certificate GMDSS GOC Almost Senior 💪
🖤 "Memorize COLREGS — all of them. You cannot Google the rules in the middle of the ocean. Sobrang dami, I know. Kaya mo yan." — Prof. Kuromi
🌐

4th Year · 2029–2030
Senior Cadet — Shipboard OJT
Your First Time at Sea for Real 🌊
6 to 12 months aboard a real vessel as a Deck Cadet. No more simulations — actual ocean, actual ship, actual responsibility. You will log sea service hours and earn your first salary at sea. This year will change you forever.
Skills you develop onboard
Bridge Watch Duties Cargo Lashing & Securing Lifeboat & Rescue Ops Engine Room Familiarization Logbook Entries Navigation Planning Mooring & Anchoring Seafarer Survival
Your First Sea Pay 💰 Sea Service Record Book Real Life Starts Here 🌊
🖤 "Malamig ang dagat. Malayo sa bahay. Mahirap. Pero pag nakita mo ang sunrise sa gitna ng karagatan — you will know exactly why you chose this. Hang in there." — Prof. Kuromi
2030–2031
PRC Board Examination
MARINA Licensure — OICNW 📜
The exam that makes everything official. Pass the PRC and MARINA board to become a licensed Officer in Charge of a Navigation Watch. This license opens every door on every ocean in the world.
Board exam coverage
Navigation & Watchkeeping Ship Stability Cargo Handling COLREGS Meteorology Maritime Law & Policy GMDSS Operations Ship Safety & Security
PRC License — Deck Officer STCW CoC Licensed Seafarer 🎓
🖤 "Review like your career depends on it — because it does. Sleep the night before. Eat breakfast. You have studied this for 4 years. Trust yourself completely." — Prof. Kuromi

Your first officer stripe. You are now responsible for firefighting equipment, safety drills, lifeboat operations, and bridge watch. You are a real professional mariner. International vessel, international pay — you earned this.

Safety Equipment Officer Fire & Abandon Ship Drills Navigation Watch 0000–0400 Chart Corrections ECDIS Operations Medical Assistance Onboard
